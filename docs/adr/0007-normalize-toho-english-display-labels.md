# Normalize TOHO English Display Labels

Easy Toho will keep TOHO Cinemas' raw English Movie Label as source data, but the Movie Card title will remove variant suffixes and normalize obvious all-caps labels into readable title case. Screening variants such as `SUB`, `DUB`, `SCREEN X`, `ATMOS`, and `MX4D` remain visible on individual showtimes so the card title stays clean without hiding format or language information.
